THE COCKPIT

Everything is modelled to detail;
There is no effort spared.

Textures are baked in with AO and indirect lighting.

Every button, switch and knob are 3D and protrude outwards or inwards. Everything can be pushed, switched or dialed in.

Systems are simulated with a custom plugin coded with FlyWithLua and are studied from the b777 FCOM. Source code can be found on GitHub.

The FMCs are custom simulated by xfmc - Performance data and vspeeds are accurate to the real 777-300ER

It is almost finished - the pilot side is almost fully furnished whilst a bit of work needs to be done on the copilot's.

BOEING


The digital simulation of the Boeing 777-300ER is a hobby I have been working on for over 24 months, it is meant to be freeware and will always remain that way. It shows my progression of a 3D modeller and coder, and each update aim to iterate or add something new.

This is in early development and therefore is not representable of what the finished product will be like.
